FAQ

Common questions.

How long is a session?
Most sessions run 90 minutes door-to-door. That gives you about 60 minutes of usable conversation, which we cut to a tight 30–45 minute episode.
What should my guest bring?
Themselves. We provide everything else — mics, lighting, water, coffee. We send a short brief 48 hours out so they know what to expect.
Can we shoot multiple episodes in one day?
Yes — most series are recorded in one or two-day blocks. Four episodes in a day is comfortable; we've done eight in a day for clients with tight schedules.
Do you publish the episode for us?
Yes if you want us to. We can publish straight to Spotify, Apple Podcasts and YouTube under your account, or hand you finished files to publish yourself.
What about off-site shoots?
We do off-site recording for events, conferences and on-location interviews. Quote is custom — same crew, just travelling. Ask on your intro call.
How quickly do you turn things around?
First cut within 48 hours of the shoot. Short-form clips and thumbnails the same week. We don't sit on your content.
